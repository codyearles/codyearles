Dinning Table

Finished February 2, 2022

This is a dinning room table I made with help from Jem for our family. We value the idea of having sit down meals toghether, espically since life can seem so fast pace and busy at times. The top is a live edge slab of Ambrosia Maple, and glass. The legs are made from heavy duty pallets and and screws, with a Yakisugi finish. The whole peice is coated in two protective layers of Tung Oil.

Finished!
