Enjoy your Garden Beds from a Hammock


I love the look and feel of a Japanese wood preservation technique called Yakisugi (焼杉), or commonly known as Shou Sugi Ban outside of Japan. This is an application of that technique.

Add Character to your Cubicle


I wanted my office desk to be a space of inspiration and productivity, so I made this desk. The top offers a glass top which drawings can be slide underneath, and can be written on with a Dry-Erace Marker for to-do lists and notes.

RVA Utility Credits with Rain Barrels


The City of RVA is incentivizing the use of rain barrels by offering a discount on your storm-water bill to aid in relieving the overburdened demand of city infrastructure. I made two that add style and functionality to my garden.
